Why and how does this exist (And what this is)

You probably heard this in the world of coding:

"Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet"

This phrase (paragraph) is from a very old story. Programmers started using it as a fast way to get example paragraphs for testing code. This phrase is very catchy, and memorable to me. So I went ahead and created a website named after it. Even though I know there are probably a hundred other websites named after this.
This exists because of a kid in middle school, riding the Tokyo metro. He writes code when he is in the metro to pass time. He is inspired by xkcd, a webcomic by Randall Munroe, who is an author and webcomic artist. This boy hopes to be the Randall Munroe for the next generation. This boy is me, the person making this blwg and posting in this blwg. I just hope that this website stands out, and shines. Right next to xkcd.
Enjoy!
